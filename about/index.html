About TaoTrails

TaoTrails is an English-language culture and travel inspiration site focused on Taoism, sacred mountains, temple etiquette, and quiet ways to understand China.

How it relates to the China travel series

TaoTrails handles culture-first discovery. ChinaPass.ai should handle arrival logistics, payment setup, transportation, city planning, and other details that change quickly.

Guardrails

  • Cultural pages should cite or verify important religious and historical claims before publication.
  • Travel logistics should be reviewed close to publication because transport, tickets, and opening rules change.
  • Health-adjacent topics such as qigong, fasting, Taoist medicine, or internal alchemy need visible educational disclaimers.
  • Living religious communities should be treated respectfully. Avoid intrusive directions or sensational framing.
